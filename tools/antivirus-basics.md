# Antivirus & Malware Basics

Malware can slow down your system and steal data.

## Signs of Malware Infection
- Slow performance
- Unexpected pop-ups
- Unknown startup programs

## Basic Steps to Handle Malware
1. Disconnect from the internet
2. Run a full antivirus scan
3. Remove suspicious applications
4. Keep your OS updated
